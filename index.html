City of Kigali

"We Will Become the Centre of Urban Excellence in Africa"

The City of Kigali aspires to become the Centre of Urban Excellence in Africa, which will be achieved through Social Inclusion, Sustainable Development, and Economic Growth. Our vision comes with imperatives in the city's planning, monitoring and compliance with national strategic agenda for sustainable development.

At the center of our key enablers sits digital technologies, which support our efforts to deliver operations and services to citizens efficiently. The CoK Inspection Hub represents our commitment to embracing innovation for proactive urban development monitoring and change detection.

Through advanced satellite technologies and Artificial Intelligence, we are building a smart, responsive city that creates better life for all our citizens and establishes Kigali as a model for sustainable urban development across Africa.
